Model Details

DistilBERT: A lightweight transformer model trained for binary classification of cyberbullying content. Processes up to 512 tokens per input with confidence scoring.

Vision Transformer: Segments images into patches and processes through transformer layers to detect harmful visual content with high precision.

Whisper: OpenAI's multilingual speech recognition model for transcribing and analyzing audio content in real-time.
